Teamwork.com is a company founded by developers, built to give our outstanding crew of programmers the best possible environment to write great code and build the world class products that our customers love. Developing great software is our DNA - it's what drives us, excites us, and it's what’s built us an incredible business.

We’re a profitable, entirely self-funded business with no investors or outside influences beyond one - our customers and what's best for them.

We are looking for developers with real passion for their work, who love learning and who would thrive in a “come see what I did” environment.

Talented developers will get to grips quickly with our code. We write CoffeeScript for Node.js, NW.js (Windows, Mac & Linux), mobile (Android & iOS), and the Web. Some of our favorite tools include: Knockout.js, jQuery, Browserify, LESS, and gulp. 

Send us on details of your projects, let us see what you’ve done in the past. If you’ve used a Teamwork Chat client, we’d love to know what you’d change or how you’d improve it.

Must Have

  • 'JavaScript: The Great Parts' - You're very accustomed to JavaScript. You craft and comment your code, but also include a duck punch / hack when needed. It's not necessary to know library X.js or framework Y.js, but you'd be completely comfortable digging into either of them because they're written in JavaScript.
  • Bring your opinions - We're continuously improving and we want you to make your voice heard; whether it may be to switch to your favourite promise library, ditch Browserify, or even just your opinion on Sublime Text.
  • Test-driven - Writing tests isn't a chore for you.
  • MySQL - You can handle writing some MySQL queries without crawling into a ball and crying.
  • Learn as you go - You have an open mind and are able to pick things up quickly. If we decide to re-write our servers in Go, we'll want your support.

Nice To Have

  • Node.js - Knowing Node would give you a headstart as our desktop clients and our servers run on it.
  • Responsive LESS - We like to write mobile-first responsive (pre-processed) CSS. We hope you do too. Mobile-Web experience would be great to have.
  • CoffeeScript
  • Open-source - We depend on and often contribute to open-source projects. If it's broken, why not fix it?
  • Show us what you got - Nothing speaks louder or shows us more about you than showing us examples of your work.
