INFRASTRUCTURE ENGINEER

Help us design better, faster infrastructure architecture.
Technical | Teamwork.com HQ, Cork, Ireland

The Job

Teamwork.com is looking for an exceptional Infrastructure Engineer/DevOps Specialist who loves making software fast, organized, and efficient.

 The role we are creating gives you the opportunity to help architect some of the infrastructure for our ground-breaking productivity software, while solving challenging technical problems.

You’ll work closely with our company founders (both developers) to implement the right solutions for ultimate scalability, durability, and performance. If this sounds like your idea of an exciting challenge. If you’re interested in helping build the infrastructure that will change the way that millions of people go about their daily business, then contact us.

On a daily basis, you’ll be fanatical about system performance. You will take ownership of our infrastructure needs through the following;

  • Help Teamwork.com products grow and scale.

  • Automate, automate, automate where possible to make our infrastructure and deployments faster and easier to maintain.

  • Develop, modify, support, and maintain AWS-based components for public facing infrastructure, application back-end, and admin and monitoring features. Maximum utilization of AWS components – we anticipate using nearly every service component available from AWS.

  • Create, maintain, and enhance the following: test, validation, and logging infrastructure for our AWS components.

  • Collaborate with our developer team to engineer creative solutions to unique challenges.

  • Prepare periodic reports on engineering activities, for the purposes of project management and resource planning.

  • Continuously work to improve product, process, and tools proficiency, for the purposes of company growth, user experience, and timely product delivery.

  • Help improve continuous integration, deployment, and security best practices.

  • Focus on keeping our applications running and scaling - you enjoy learning, deploying, and scaling new technologies

  • Experience of managing large server deployments.

  • Develop, modify, support, and maintain AWS-based services for public facing infrastructure, application back-end, and admin and monitoring features.

  • Create, maintain, and enhance the following: test, validation, and logging infrastructure for our AWS components.

  • Continuously work to improve product, process, and tools proficiency, for the purposes of company growth, user experience, and timely product delivery.

  • Help improve continuous integration, deployment, and security best practices.

Must Have

  • You have a preference for automation, but understand that sometimes it will be better to do it by hand.

  • You want to understand how our applications work so that you can optimize their delivery.

  • 3+ years of AWS experience (general DevOps/WS/DB/infrastructure).

  • Practical experience with most, if not all, components of AWS: EC2, Beanstalk, ELB, Route53, S3, Cloudfront, SNS, SWF, SQS, RDS, DynamoDB, ElastiCache, IAM, CloudFormation.

  • Hands-on experience with monitoring tools, intrusion detection mechanisms, and vulnerability assessment tools and practices.

  • General scripting wizardry and programming experience in Go (Golang).

  • Strong Linux skills.

  • Strong MySQL query, optimization, and maintenance skills.

  • Self-motivated team player who demonstrates initiative and flexibility.

  • Strong organizational skills, with the ability to handle and prioritize multiple tasks.

  • Friendly with the right attitude. Smart and gets things done. Can work with others and communicate well.

 

Nice To Have

Beyond the requirements above, the preferred candidate will have:

– SQL and NoSQL experience.

– Familiarity with various flavors of SQL/NoSQL.

– Experience designing solutions for access control, user authentication, and service security in general.

– Strong verbal and written communication skills.

– Experience with Redis optimization techniques.

Working at Teamwork.com isn't like a normal job. We believe in hiring only the best and then looking after them like they deserve. On top of a great financial package, we do everything possible to make Teamwork.com a great place to work.
