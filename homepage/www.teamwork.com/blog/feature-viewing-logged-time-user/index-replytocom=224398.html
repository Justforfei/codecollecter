New Feature: Viewing Logged Time for a User

Posted January 29, 2015

From the very beginning Teamwork.com’s time tracking has been one of our most popular features. We’re always looking at new tweaks and features that we can add to make this even more helpful. This month we’re adding the ability to visualise the hours that any particular user has logged. Its great for a quick glance to see who’s putting in the hours, what days are the busiest, what hours are billable etc.

To access this new feature we’ve added a new Logged Time tab to the User Profile section. You can access it from the People section at the top bar of your Teamwork Project Manager account, or by clicking on your user icon in the upper right corner.
