Teamwork Desk – October Updates

Posted October 30, 2015

This month has by far been the busiest in Teamwork Desk’s history. The team has been working night and day on this month’s updates. We have been so eager to announce the news of these bad boys. Now the wait is finally over, get ready to be amazed!

Natively Responsive on Mobile and Tablet

Teamwork Desk now adjusts beautifully to fit your screen. You can use the intuitive responsive Teamwork Desk with ease on any device. Take a look for yourself…

Mobile Apps

Teamwork Desk is just days away from releasing an app for your iPhone. Supporting your customers will no longer mean you have to be stuck in the office. With Teamwork Desk iPhone App, you’ll be able to help your customers from just about anywhere. Check out this video to see how it will work.

In-Browser Web Notifications

Announcing browser notifications! To enable these, go into your profile and click Enable Web Notifications. Once that’s done, your notifications will pop up in your browser. These notifications use the same preferences as your Agent Notifications, but now you don’t have to jump into your e-mail to keep up to date.

Improved Search

We have radically improved the search function in Teamwork Desk. Searching for tickets will now take a fraction of one second. No really, time it! The UI looks the same, but the accuracy and speed of your search results have dramatically improved and are now blazing fast.

Customer Portals

What could be more important that talking to your customers? Now, we’re giving you the opportunity to form better relationships with the people who mean the most. Customer Portals will give your customers the ability to log into Teamwork Desk and obtain the status of their own tickets. This will improve your interaction and engagement with your customers. Increased levels of communication will massively increase your customer’s satisfaction. The Teamwork Desk team is made up of perfectionists and they are still tweaking one or two things, but they can’t wait to show you what Customer Portals can do! Watch this space. It won’t be long now.

We’ve had an influx of feedback from our customers. Click here to read some interviews with some Teamwork Desk fans. We’ve also put together a blog post filled with some of our own top tips for Teamwork Desk power users (if you’re not already a power user, you will be when you know these). 

Have fun playing around with the new features! As always, if you have any questions on anything Teamwork Desk related, just shoot us an email at support@teamwork.com, or leave a comment down below.
