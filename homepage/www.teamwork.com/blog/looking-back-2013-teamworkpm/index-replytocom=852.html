Behind the Scenes, Features & Updates

Looking back on 2013 at Teamwork.com

Posted December 23, 2013

Every year we work hard to ensure we keep delivering the same great service you expect from us. One thing we always pride ourselves on is our continual updates and improvements to the app. 2013 has seen us reach some amazing milestones, and it’s been one of our biggest years ever at Teamwork.com. 

To finish off the year, we thought it would be great to look back on some of the highlights and features rolled out into Teamwork Project Manager in 2013.

JANUARY

January started with a bang as it marked the release of our first ever iPad app. The new app has a beautiful panel design which allows you to smoothly sweep across your iPad’s screen to move between projects.

Read January Newsletter

FEBRUARY

This month saw the release of real-time updates. This allowed customers to see changes to tasks instantly when other team members were working on the same page. It also meant all changes were instant and no longer required a reload in order to see them. February saw big updates to default notifications on projects as well as the ability to now email notebooks into projects.

Read February Newsletter

MARCH

In March one of the most requested updates ever to Teamwork was launched: Gantt charts were finally released to all users.  The arrival of Gantt charts gave people a new tool to help with planning, re-scheduling and the ability to visualise projects in a way that had never been possible before.

Read March Newsletter

APRIL

It was going to be hard to top the release of Gantt charts from the month before, but April was equally impressive. This month saw one of the other biggest feature requests ever released: Sub-tasks. April also saw an update pushed to the iPad app to support the newly released sub-tasks.

Read April Newsletter

MAY

After two crazy months, we spent May focusing on existing features and improving them. Updates were made to the Milestones section as well as sub-task support being added to the Gantt chart, along with a large number of other tweaks and bug fixes.

Read May Newsletter

JUNE

In June we tackled a feature that had been around for quite a while: Task List templates. The update really took this feature to a whole new level by adding in the ability to create roles for tasks and also allowing more flexibility with assigning dates to tasks.

Read June Newsletter

JULY

We may have had one of the best summers ever in Ireland but this didn’t stop us pushing on to roll out another great enhancement. July saw the introduction of drag and drop file upload along with the ability to also Like a file. The daily email was given a complete overhaul as well.

Read July Newsletter

AUGUST

August was a month where we really focused on bugs and other minor updates to the app. During this month we also rolled out a nice update to the harvest timers. We also started on the groundwork behind the scenes to allow for the upcoming big UI change.

Read August Newsletter

SEPTEMBER

In September, one of the biggest updates and changes ever in Teamwork history was released: the new look Teamwork. The new design change also allowed for big changes to the notifications centre as well as improvements to many areas throughout the app, including search, notebooks and a whole new project overview.

Read September Newsetter

OCTOBER

This month we spent a lot of time tweaking and updating features from the UI change based on feedback we received. We also released a new project activity report as well as added in a new All Files page to help you track all files across all projects.

Read October Newsletter

NOVEMBER

After many re-writes and versions, we were delighted to introduce the brand new iPhone and Android apps for Teamwork. November also marked the release of the new WYSIWYG editor option for comments and messages as well as the incredibly handy email links into a project feature as well.

Read November Newsletter

DECEMBER

We have finally come to the end of 2013. It’s been an amazing year with a huge amount of updates and new features released here at Teamwork. Thanks to you all for all your feedback and support throughout the year. We have some more amazing updates and ground breaking features planned for the new year, so make sure you keep an eye on this blog along with our Facebook page to stay up to date with all of our latest news.
