Teamwork Projects for Android

Cleaner, faster and a modern feel. Our new Android app makes it even easier to keep your projects moving, wherever you have to be.

The Dashboard

Clean, crisp and a breeze to use, our brand new Android App makes it faster and easier to get your work done. Designed and built from scratch with new features and faster, more intuitive navigation, the new app is the most powerful Teamwork mobile app to date.
Here's a taste of some of the things you can do:

  • Keep an eye on everything that's happened with Latest Activity
  • Check your, or everyone's, tasks using multiple filters
  • Keep on top of the upcoming Milestones, and the late ones too!
  • Find out the status of everyone you work with
  • Quickly add, delete or swap between multiple accounts

Inside a Project?

Select a project and you'll find everything you need to keep it on track when you're out and about. Want to know what's been going on while you were in the meeting? Check the project activity: a quick skim and you're right back up to speed and ready to create tasks, send messages and upload photos wherever you happen to be.

  • Latest Activity

    Anything that happens anywhere in your project shows up here, so it's a great place to go when you want to do a quick check of what's been happening. From here you can tap on any item to go directly to them without having to navigate to their project first. For example:

    • Keep an eye on everything that's happened with Latest Activity
    • Check your, or everyone's, tasks using multiple filters
    • Keep on top of the upcoming Milestones, and the late ones too!

  • Tasks And Task Lists

    Tasks and Task Lists provide the structure for the project, detailing everything that needs to be done and who needs to do it. Our mobile app is streamlined to make working with tasks simple and efficient.

    • Create tasks for any project, straight from the Dashboard
    • View just today's Tasks
    • View upcoming tasks
    • View late tasks
    • View everyone's tasks or just your own
    • Break complicated tasks down into sub-tasks
    • Edit, assign, set priority
    • Log time spent on a particular task
    • Add photos
    • Secure: comments for private tasks are only sent to the appropriate people

  • Messages

    The mobile app provides all of the messaging features that you'd expect, in a subtle and powerful package.

    • Easy to read layout
    • Increased security for private messages
    • View inline attachments
    • Upload photos
    • Smart automatic recipient selection

  • Milestones

    Keep track of important dates in the life of your project.

    • Check important details such as when it was last changed and by whom
    • Create new Milestones and edit existing ones
    • Add a comment
    • Check back on old completed Milestones if necessary

  • Files

    Wherever you are, you can quickly access all of the files associated with your project.

    • Preview all of the files associated with the project
    • View files that were linked in from Dropbox, Google Drive or Box
    • Upload your own photos

  • Notebooks

    Notebooks are great for gathering together free form notes, ideas, decisions and images in one place. The mobile app allows you to view your Notebooks exactly as they appear on the desktop.

    • View Notebooks
    • View all associated comments
    • Add your own comment

  • People

    The key to any company or project is the people that work in it. When you need to get in touch with someone urgently, the mobile app gets you connected quickly.

    • Tap on a phone number to start a call
    • Hit the email address to send them a message
    • Get in contact with everyone associated with your company
    • Check who's working on a project
    • Check extra details such as the person's role
    • Color coded Contacts

  • Time

    Whether you're tracking time to bill a client, or simply to review and improve your own processes, the mobile app makes it easy to review and log time.

    • Add time to projects
    • Record time spent on individual tasks
    • Choose from a set of easy to use filters
    • View totals for Billed and Unbilled time

  • Search

    You know that somebody put something, somewhere, but you just can't remember where exactly. Hit search and start typing, if it's anywhere in Teamwork Projects then search will find it for you.

    • Choose what should be searched: only Notebooks, for example
    • Fast: only the minimum information sent
    • Works across all of your projects
