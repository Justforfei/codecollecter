Teamwork Desk – August Updates

Posted August 31, 2015

It’s a big month for Teamwork Desk users as the team continues to roll out more and more features designed to help you deliver true customer happiness!

Time Tracking

Time Tracking has arrived in Teamwork Desk. In your open ticket, you will notice a timer that begins timing from the moment the ticket is opened until the time you mark it as on-hold, solved, or closed.  This latest addition means that you can get reports for automatic and manual time tracked on tickets. Teamwork Desk users will have a better overall view of what tickets are taking the team the longest to answer, meaning you’ll know when and where you need to dedicate more resources to a specific case.

 

Even More Triggers

Last month, we announced the release of Triggers on Teamwork Desk which, so far, has been really well received by our users. Since that release, the team has been busy with new additions and improvements to the Triggers feature.  These include:

  • Conditions – users can now apply conditions for ticket types, e.g. support request or marketing request as well as unassigned tickets. Where tickets have been left unassigned, users can now apply an action to them. Conditions now apply to the ticket source also.
  • See which user has created the ticket.
  • Users can allow html formatting for “send an e-mail” action.
  • Add a condition: body of first e-mail contains... This means that now the search for a keyword applies to the body of the e-mail instead of just the headline. E.g Where the word “discount” appears in the e-mail body then your condition will apply.

In addition to the above changes we have made some other adjustments that:

  • Allow users to add a ticket from Teamwork Desk to an existing task in Teamwork Projects.
  • Gives users the option to lock down Helpdocs access to specific IP address/ranges.
  • Sorting Helpdocs articles is now available.
  • We have also adjusted our pricing.

That’s it for Teamwork Desk for the month of August. More to follow in September, including something very, very awesome updates that we know you’ll love!
