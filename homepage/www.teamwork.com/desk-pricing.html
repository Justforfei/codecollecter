Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel, upgrade, or downgrade later?

Yes, of course - Teamwork Desk is a monthly service and you can cancel, upgrade, or downgrade any time.

Which methods of payment do you accept?

Our Monthly Subscriptions can be pay with Credit Card: Visa, Mastercard or Maestro. If your organization does not allow Credit Card payments, please contact us on supportteamwork.com to arrange an alternative payment method.
For our Annual Subscriptions we can issue you with an invoice and accept payment via Bank Transfer-This works for every plan except for Startup.

Why are you giving us 30 days free?

We want you to be 100% happy before parting with your cash so feel free to create a site with no obligations. All our paid plans include a 30-day free trial and we don't mention payment details until your trial is nearing the end.

Do you offer discounts to non-profit organizations or educational bodies?

We deal with each case individually depending on the type of organization. Please contact supportteamwork.com outlining the type of organization you are, and we would be happy to help you.

When do I pay?

Near the end of your 30-day free trial, you will receive an email asking you to pay to continue using Teamwork Desk.
At this time, you can choose to cancel with no hassle or obligation. If you decide to continue using Teamwork Desk, you will be asked to create a Paid Subscription for the monthly payment.

Do I pay monthly or annually?

Your credit card will be charged automatically every month. Again, you can cancel at any time. If you prefer to pay annually offline, you can contact us and we will be happy to help. There are no cancellation or other hidden fees!

What if I just paid and decide to upgrade to a higher plan?

No problem. Simply click the "Upgrade" button under the new plan and agree to the new price plan. Your new pricing plan will not deduct payment from your account until the next month's billing cycle.

Have more questions?

Our customer support team is here to help!
