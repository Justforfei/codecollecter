Keeping Up with Teamwork Chat

Posted April 29, 2015

There is no doubt that it has been a productive few months here at Teamwork.com HQ. Now that we’re a three-product company, we’re learning new ways to juggle the monthly updates across our product offering for our users. In the midst of the furor around Teamwork Desk last month, we didn’t bring you any updates on Teamwork Chat. We didn’t forget or anything but we believed that Teamwork Desk deserved its moment in the spotlight too!

The Teamwork Chat team has been working quietly behind the scenes since they launched back in February. This month, they rolled out two new updates and have fixed some small issues that users were having. As always, we welcome users’ feedback. One feature that has been the subject of discussion is the ability to make Teamwork Chat accessible to people outside the owner company.  We’re getting conflicting feedback on this as some want that feature to stay the way it is, while others want it to change.  For some, the idea of having clients and others outside the company in constant contact with their team isn’t appealing. We are working on it though and, in true Teamwork.com style, we’re looking for a way that will make everyone, so just bear with us on that one!

Coming down the line very soon, you can expect to see Android and iOS Apps, the web version of Teamwork Chat and Independent sign-ups as well as better Windows desktop notifications. Since their trip to the Fluent Conference in San Francisco, the guys have become even more inspired than before. While there, our Product Lead Adam wrote a blog entitled “Under the Hood of Teamwork Chat.” It’s a bit technical and will cause the non-techies among us to spontaneously combust, but nonetheless feel free to have a read. One final thing, we’re expanding the Teamwork Chat team too. We’re looking for awesome developers who want to help us continue to build an awesome product. We’ll continue to roll out the updates and keep you posted in next month’s newsletter but in the meantime, we hope you’re enjoying using Teamwork Chat.

Chat to you soon (See what we did there?!)

The Teamwork Chat Team.
