Bank accounts Aggregation

Bankin’s API is the leading European solution for aggregation of bank accounts. Our proprietary technology allows you to retrieve data from different bank accounts (Personal and Business) in just a few seconds.

Software publishers, accounting firms, banks, credit and insurance companies and start-ups all use Bankin’s API to automatize banking data retrieval from their respective customer bases (for Personal and/or Business accounts).

Our API allows you to retrieve standardized banking data such as account balances, specific transactions and their associated categories (Transportation, Restaurant etc.) and detailed reports from savings and mortgage accounts.
More than 350 banks are currently available, and more are being added. Our API is highly secure and reliable. Bankin' uses the same API to deliver secure banking data to over 1.2 million users via its mobile applications each day.

Exemple : Software publisher, Accounting firms, Fintech startup etc.

Banking Data analysis

Bankin’s technologies allow you to analyze and identify incomes and spendings categories across very large amounts of transaction data, and this with high precision via our API.

This high-level of precision is obtained by leveraging the million+ reach of our current community. We are continuously learning from the analysis of our users' behaviors, which enables us to build aggregated and anonymized data segments and develop an extremely precise database. For example, uncategorized transactions marked as 'unknown' will be categorized by our users and by doing so, they help us to enrich and enhance Bankin's collective intelligence database.
