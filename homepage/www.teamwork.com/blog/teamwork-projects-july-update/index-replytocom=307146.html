Teamwork Projects – July Updates

Posted July 31, 2015

There’s plenty going on with Teamwork Projects this month. Let’s have a look at what the team has been up to.

Mobile

Among about one hundred other bug fixes and small feature enhancement, the Teamwork Mobile team is particularly excited about the fact that you can now upload files, photos, and links directly to your Teamwork Projects phone app. It’s really easy to do, but even we created a video to help get you started with this new feature.

Files

Zip File Download

Managing large numbers of files in Teamwork Projects just got a whole lot easier! Files, comments, and messages can now be downloaded as a zip.

Go to the file section in a project, press options, and click the select multiple files button.

Select all the files you want to save and press download. Alternatively, you could use one of the Teamwork Projects shortcuts and press the “M” key on your computer this will act as a “Multiple Download” function. Again, you just select all the files you want to save and then press download.

 

Boom. All of the files will download together in a zip and you won’t have to waste time downloading individual files!

Shareable links

You always had the ability to share files on Teamwork Projects, but now we’ve made it even easier for you! No longer do you have to open up the file to share it. Just go into your files and on the right-hand side of each one you will see a drop-down arrow button. Click on it and you’ll find the share option.

 

You’ll see a link appear just copy and paste it and share with your colleagues.

 

This streamlines the whole process and saves you time so that you can spend it on the things that really matter, like collaborating with your team and getting stuff done!

Custom Time Reports based on Tags

In May, we released Tags on Tasks.  In June, we announced Tags on Everything. It seems to be the feature that just keeps on giving! This month, we’ve combined Tags with another feature: Time Reports. You can now filter your Time Reports with Tags. Get the exact information you are looking for and customize Time Reports to your specific needs.

Billing API

We’ve been working hard on developing a Billing API for your invoices and expenses. Look at all the cool stuff you can do now:

Invoices

  • Get all invoices across your projects
  • Get all invoices on a single project
  • Get a single invoice
  • Create a new invoice in a project
  • Update a specific invoice
  • Delete a specific invoice
  • Mark a specific invoice as complete
  • Mark a specific invoice as not complete
  • Get a list of valid currency codes

Expenses

  • Get all expenses across your projects
  • Get all expenses on a single project
  • Get a single expense
  • Create a new expense in a project
  • Update a single expense
  • Delete a single expense

It’s been another busy month for the team. If you have any questions, simply drop us a line to support@teamwork.com.
