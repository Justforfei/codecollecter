Teamwork Chat starts talking…

Posted March 31, 2015

We’ve shared with you our ultimate vision of software within your business that really integrates and talks to each other that really helps your business be better and do more.  We honestly believe that at the core of how to run your business better is real-time communication and that’s why we built Teamwork Chat to be at the core of your business and it’s also why we made it completely free to use – you can’t put a price or limits on any teamworker’s ability to interact with their team. That’s also why, even though it is free software, we have a brand new, dedicated Teamwork Chat team here at Teamwork.com that will continue to build out new features and improve the old ones based on your suggestions.

This month, as well as a host of tweaks and performance improvements, we’re delighted to announce the first steps of what will eventually be total, seamless integration between Teamwork Chat and its siblings Teamwork Projects and our new Teamwork Desk products. With the latest release of Teamwork Chat you can turn any message into a task in your Teamwork Project installation, simply by right clicking on the message and following the instructions.

We’ve also enabled our Cards feature which means that it you copy the URL of any Teamwork Project’s Task or Teamwork Desk Ticket into a conversation, we’ll pull the relevant information into chat for you in the form of a nice graphical card.

These are just to first steps of some very exciting integration plans for Teamwork Chat. Watch this space.

Click here to start getting real-time chatting and collaborating with your team.
