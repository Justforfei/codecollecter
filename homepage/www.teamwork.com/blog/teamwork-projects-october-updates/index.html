Teamwork Projects – October Updates

Posted October 30, 2015

This month is all about Mobile! The team has been working hard on some really exciting new features this month for your iOS and Android apps.

Push Notifications

We are delighted to announce that the wait is over… Push notifications on iOS and Android apps have arrived! No matter where you are you can get updates in real time and always be kept in the loop.

Android App

The Android App had a big release with the first half of its material overhaul. This is a big jump in the look and feel of the app – it’s so much better now.

Today Widget Updates

Plan your day better with the new Today Widget updates. You can now flip between your Due and Recently Assigned Tasks in the Today Widget. This is going to make your life so much easier. It’s the quickest and simplest way to keep on top of your work. With this new feature, you can easily check your daily workload and plan your schedule before you even come into the office!

Halloween Special

Just for fun we’ve snuck some Halloween emoji’s into your apps. Take a look at your upcoming tasks ;)!

The Secret Project

The Teamwork Projects Team have been busy working on a secret project this month. It’s not quite finished yet but we can’t wait to show you what we’ve been up to! It will blow your mind! Stay tuned for more updates coming your way very soon!

It’s been a big month for Mobile! We hope you enjoy all of the new features and improvements! If you have any questions on any of the above features feel free to comment below, or alternatively send us an email to support@teamwork.com. Don’t forget to stay tuned for upcoming updates!
