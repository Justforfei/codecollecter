Sub-tasks now display in the Gantt chart

Posted May 31, 2013

The last two months have seen some of our most popular updates ever released for TeamworkPM. First it was Gantt charts which so many of you had been waiting years for, and then came Sub-tasks, again a massive and long awaited update.

Well, this month we are proud to announce the two have been combined. One of the biggest requests from you all this month has been the ability to see sub-tasks on the Gantt chart. We are very happy to say this is now possible. You can now view and edit sub-tasks in the TeamworkPM Gantt chart.

Sub tasks now display in the Gantt chart

As you can see from the screen shot,  the sub-tasks are indented so you know they are sub-tasks. The dependencies also appear automatically for sub-tasks, as a top-level task can’t be completed until its sub-tasks are finished.

Enjoy, and if you have any feedback on this, just let us know via the feedback and support link at the bottom of your account.
