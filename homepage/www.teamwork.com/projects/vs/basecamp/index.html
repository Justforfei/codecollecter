We have the essential features that Basecamp
is missing, including:

Time Tracking

Teamwork Projects has excellent time tracking features, a free time tracking desktop widget for PC and Mac, billing features, and reporting.

Sub-tasks

Be even more organized by occasionally breaking your tasks into smaller chunks.

Recurring Tasks

Easily and quickly create recurring tasks to help take the pain out of managing your projects.

Tasks for Multiple People

In the real world, it often doesn't matter who does a task, so long as it gets done. Or you may need each person to work on the task. We cater for both scenarios.

Advanced Privacy

The privacy feature within Teamwork Projects is called "Lockdown." Lockdown gives you the ability to select who can view project items including tasks, messages, files, comments, and notebooks.

Nicer Site Address

In Basecamp Next, your site address looks like https://basecamp.com/1756170/ - horrible, eh? In Teamwork Projects, it looks like company.teamwork.com.

Task Priorities

Tasks can be low, medium, or high priority - you'll wonder how you ever lived without this.

Task Dependencies

Need to get task "A" done before you can start on task "B"? No problem with Teamwork Projects.

Gantt Charts

Teamwork Projects now has beautiful, powerful, and printable interactive Gantt charts.

We Care

You'll get fast, amazing support from people who really care. We actually listen to all the suggestions we receive. A lot of our features were driven by people just like you, letting us know what they would love to see.

Custom Branding

Customize your Teamwork Projects site with your colors and logo. Choose a nice, easy to remember URL like yourcompany.teamwork.com. Or use your own domain, such as http://projects.yoursite.com - This looks great to your customers.

Attach files to tasks

Attach required files directly to your tasks. No more saying, "please refer to the file I uploaded into the project or on a discussion." With Teamwork Projects, the files are attached directly to the tasks and even to the comments.

Apps For iOS & Android

Completely free, beautiful apps, made by us, and continually updated. We have apps for iPhone/Android/iPad and HTML5.

Task Lists Templates

Because entering the same list of tasks again and again is a pain. We've made it easy for you to set up your own predefined lists.

Transparent Roadmap

Unlike others, at Teamwork.com we publish our product roadmap. We are continually improving and we listen to your suggestions.

Official Integrations

Teamwork Projects has beautiful integrations with Dropbox, Box.com, Google Docs, Freshbooks, Harvest, and more.

Happiest Customers

Our customers are our number one priority. We could tell you why we think you should chose Teamwork Projects, but you don't have to take our word for it! Instead, check out some of the testimonials below and see what our customers think.

Tags on Everything

Tags give you a new way to keep track of your work. You can Tag or label everything. Filter your Tasks, Projects, Milestones, Files, Messages, and even Notebooks with colored Tags to see what you have to focus on at any given time. This is going to make your searching and reporting so easy!
